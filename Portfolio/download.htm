About University of Chester

Built as the first teacher training college in the United Kingdom, The University of Chester seeks to provide all students with the education, skills, support and motivation to enable them to develop as confident world citizens. The university’s foundational values are on recognizing “the dignity and worth of every individual, the vital role of education in the service of society and , the inherent value of the pursuit of truth and freedom of enquiry.”

Unique Fact

Work based learning modules are optional courses students can take at the University of Chester which involves spending 5 weeks in the summer working for an organisation and producing a reflective report that focuses on their skills development. Additionally, students can work towards Chester Difference Award which has criteria based on a points system for extracurricular participation, employability training and work/volunteer experience.
